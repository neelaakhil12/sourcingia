Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Sourcingia Management Consulting Private Limited*, a company incorporated under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office in India, (Sourcingia )respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of candidates, clients, employees, and website visitors.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information such as contact details, resumes, employment history, educational qualifications, professional details, and other recruitment-related information to provide staffing, recruitment, talent acquisition, and human resource consulting services.

3. Sources of Information

Information may be collected directly from individuals, through our website, job portals, referrals, professional networking platforms, or other lawful sources.

4. How We Use Information

We use this information to evaluate candidate suitability, match candidates with employment opportunities, manage client relationships, conduct verification processes, fulfill contractual obligations, comply with applicable laws and regulations, and improve our services.

5. Information Sharing & Disclosure

Personal information may be shared with prospective employers, clients, background verification agencies, authorized service providers, and government authorities where required by law.

Sourcingia does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Security & Retention

We maintain appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, loss, or misuse and retain such information only for as long as necessary for legitimate business, legal, and regulatory purposes.

7. Consent and Compliance

By submitting your information to Sourcingia Management Consulting Private Limited or using our services, you consent to the collection, use, storage, and processing of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws, including the provisions of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, where applicable.

8. Privacy Queries & Contact

For any privacy-related queries, requests, or concerns, please contact us through our official communication channels.
